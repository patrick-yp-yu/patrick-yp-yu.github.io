Unity Game Project - Training99

A bullet-hell action game but not just dodging.

Introduction

Challenge your hand-eye coordination! This is a bullet-hell action game but not just dodging. Players should dodge bullets from all directions to score.

Try to use color-matching & shape-matching mechanisms to find your power-up abilities.

Mode Gaming Goal
Endless Dodge bullets in all directions, keep alive as long as you can.
Level Solve tasks to get a key, and get out of each escape room.
Versus Live longer than the other player/AI.
