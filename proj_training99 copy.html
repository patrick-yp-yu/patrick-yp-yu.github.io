What is this game about?

This is a bullet-hell action game but not just dodging. Using color-matching & shape-matching mechanisms, we have three types of gaming modes:level mode, endless mode, and versus mode to challenge players’ hand-eye coordination. Players should dodge bullets from all directions to score. Trying to not be shot, the longer the better.

How do I play this game?

Dodge bullets that have a different color from yours.

Mode Gaming Goal
Endless Dodge bullets in all directions, keep alive as long as you can
Level Solve tasks to get a key, and get out of each escape room
Versus Live longer than the other player/AI
